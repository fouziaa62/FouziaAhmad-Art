About Me

Fouzia Ahmad

Hi, I'm Fouzia Ahmad, an artist based on earth. I specialize in abstract art, portraits, and beauties. I have been painting for over 10 years and have showcased my work in various exhibitions.

My inspiration comes from nature, emotions, and everyday life. I love to experiment with colors, textures, and techniques to create unique pieces of art.

Feel free to explore my collection and get in touch if you have any questions or would like to purchase a painting.
