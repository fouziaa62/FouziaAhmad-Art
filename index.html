About Me

Fouzia Ahmad

Hi, I'm Fouzia Ahmad, an artist based on earth. I specialize in abstract art, portraits, and beauties. I have been painting for over 20 years and have showcased my work in various exhibitions. My journey as an artist began at a young age, and over the years, I have honed my skills and developed a unique style that reflects my passion for creativity. Each piece of art I create is a reflection of my thoughts, emotions, and experiences. I believe that art has the power to connect people and evoke deep emotions, and I strive to achieve that through my work.

My inspiration comes from nature, emotions, and everyday life. I love to experiment with colors, textures, and techniques to create unique pieces of art.

Feel free to explore my collection and get in touch if you have any questions or would like to have a painting.
